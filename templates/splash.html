TolaData combines ICT and M&E Systems with Open Data and Open Source tools to provide a dynamic platform for data discovery and high-impact analysis.

Tola Activity

TolaActivity is a collection of standard data forms, reports, and visualizations designed to track the impact of organizations' programs and projects. There are two primary sections of Tola Activity: Project Workflow and Indicator Tracking.

Tola Tables

TolaTables is a data collection, aggregation and management tool for all types of data collection methods designed to track the impact of NGO programs and projects in the field.

Tola Work

TolaWork is a helpdesk, documentation and support tool for TolaData
